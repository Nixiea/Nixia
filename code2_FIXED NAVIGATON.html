The Story Behind Francia’s Perfume

Many perfumes smell amazing, but for people with sensitive noses, they can be too strong. Strong perfumes can cause dizziness, headache, sneezing, and allergy triggers. Because of this, Francia’s Perfume was created: light, gentle fragrances that make people feel fresh and comfortable.
