About Me


Hi, I’m Shathlaine Nicole S. Paragas! I’m 18 years old, born on September 14, 2007, and I’m from Caloocan City, Philippines. I love exploring life, discovering new things, and sharing a little piece of my world with others. I’m the kind of girl with big dreams and a heart for helping others, always wanting to bring happiness to the people who matter most in my life.

I’d describe myself as extroverted, creative, curious, and generous. I love singing even if I don’t have the strongest voice, and I’m very organized and I enjoy keeping things tidy and neat. Music is a big part of my life, and I’m always willing to try new experiences and learn new things.

My biggest goals are to finish my studies and build a stable, fulfilling career. I also dream of traveling to different countries and bringing my whole family with me so they can experience the life they deserve. I believe my loved ones deserve all the good things in the world, and I hope to make that a reality one day.

This site is my little corner to share who I am, what I love, and the journey I’m on. I hope it gives you a glimpse into my life and maybe even inspires you along the way.

Favorite Foods

I’m a true food lover who enjoys almost everything, but fries, fried chicken, and pizza are always at the top of my list. I also appreciate simple yet comforting dishes like ampalaya with egg and anything served with warm, flavorful soup. I love fruits such as pears, oranges, dalandan, watermelon, melon, mangoes, and mangosteen, among many others, because their fresh, sweet, and juicy flavors always make me happy. I have a sweet tooth as well—candies are one of my simple joys, and I adore desserts like buko salad, graham cake, leche flan, ube treats, donuts, brownies, and more. Most of all, I cherish home-cooked meals, especially when my grandfather or grandmother prepares them, because food made with love always tastes the best.

Drink Experiences

I love brunch dates, chocolate treats, coffee breaks, baking, and cooking. There’s something special about preparing food and sharing it with others, and even simple meals can feel meaningful to me. I enjoy refreshing drinks like Sprite, Nestea, different kinds of fruit juices, and of course water, which is my everyday essential. I’m not as into milk tea as I used to be, but I still treat myself to fruit tea since it has become my preferred choice. Whether it’s a cozy café moment, homemade baked goods, or a simple drink on a warm day, food and drinks always bring me comfort and happiness.

Self-Care Moments

Getting a massage and having quiet “me-time” are important to me. Taking care of myself helps me recharge, stay balanced, and clear my mind. I love buying things that make me feel confident and stunning, whether it’s body care or skincare products. I especially enjoy investing in skincare for my face because I believe my “face card” should never decline. I also love fixing myself up and doing my makeup since it boosts my confidence and makes me feel more empowered. For me, self-care is not just about looking good, but also about feeling good from the inside out. It’s my way of showing love and appreciation to myself.

Cozy Living & Simple Comforts

I love snuggling under a warm blanket, sleeping in a freshly made bed, taking warm showers, and wearing cozy clothes. Little comforts like these make me feel safe, relaxed, and at peace. I absolutely love cleaning as well because it gives me a sense of order and makes my space feel fresh and welcoming. I also enjoy organizing my things, lighting scented candles, playing soft music, and creating a calm, cozy atmosphere at home. For me, comfort and coziness are not just about relaxation, but also about surrounding myself with warmth, calm, and little joys that make life feel extra nice.

Spicy Food

I really don’t like spicy food because it hurts my ears and makes me uncomfortable. Strong and overwhelming flavors don’t suit me, and I prefer foods that are mild, soothing, and easy on my senses. Spicy meals make eating stressful rather than enjoyable.

Public Speaking

Public speaking makes me nervous and anxious. I feel much more comfortable expressing myself in smaller groups or private settings rather than in front of a crowd. I often worry about forgetting what to say or being judged, and large audiences can be overwhelming for me. I prefer meaningful conversations where I can take my time to think and respond, rather than rushed or formal presentations. Over time, I’m learning to build confidence, but I still feel more at ease sharing my thoughts in calm, supportive environments.

Environmental Annoyances

I dislike bugs, humidity, and all kinds of pollution, especially air pollution, since I have allergic rhinitis. Messy or dirty environments also make me uneasy. I appreciate clean, organized, and calm spaces that allow me to feel comfortable and at ease.

Social Pet Peeves

Loud talking, people being rude or tardy, and those constantly on their phones bother me. People who don’t return smiles or fail to acknowledge others’ efforts can be draining to be around. I appreciate kindness, patience, and thoughtfulness, and I notice when these qualities are missing in social interactions.

Social Pressure & Crowds

Small talk, being forced into large crowds, and social expectations to reach certain milestones by a specific age make me anxious. I prefer meaningful conversations and spaces where I feel comfortable and respected. Too much pressure or a busy, noisy crowd can make me feel overwhelmed and stressed.

Dishonesty & Negativity

I strongly dislike liars, gossiping, and constant complaining. People who focus only on negativity without trying to improve or solve problems can drain my energy. I value honesty, integrity, and a positive mindset, and being around negative or deceitful people makes me uncomfortable.

Minor Physical Discomforts

Things like itchy clothing, sticky hands, uncomfortable shoes, or other small physical irritations can ruin my mood. I like to feel clean, comfortable, and at ease with my body and surroundings.
