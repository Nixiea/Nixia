OLIVIA RODRIGO

BASIC FACTS

  • Full name: Olivia Isabel Rodrigo
  • Born: February 20, 2003 (age 23)
  • Birthplace: Murrieta, California (raised in Temecula)
  • Heritage: Filipino-American (father Filipino, mother German/Irish)
  • Nationality: American
  • Occupation: Singer-songwriter, actress
  • Started as child actress on Disney (Bizaardvark, High School Musical: The Musical: The Series)
  • Breakout hit: "drivers license" (2021)
  • Height: 5'5" (165 cm)

AWARDS & ACHIEVEMENTS

  • 3× Grammy Awards winner (including Best New Artist 2022)
  • 14 Grammy nominations total (as of 2026)
  • Won Best Pop Vocal Album (SOUR), Best Pop Solo Performance ("drivers license")
  • Multiple Billboard Music Awards & other wins
  • Named Time Entertainer of the Year (2021)
  • Received UMG x Reverb Amplifier Award for Positive Change (2026)
  • Youngest artist to debut at #1 on Billboard Hot 100 multiple times
  • SOUR album reached 250+ weeks on Billboard 200 (milestone in 2026)

MUSIC & CAREER

  • Debut album: SOUR (2021) – massive breakout
  • Second album: GUTS (2023) – hit singles like "vampire"
  • Working on third album (OR3) – teased as "sad love songs" with joyful/dark twist (expected 2026)
  • Known for raw, emotional songwriting about heartbreak, growth, love & vulnerability
  • Inspired by artists like Taylor Swift, plays piano & guitar
  • Started songwriting young; proud of Filipino roots & family traditions
  • Advocates for positive change (environment, fan engagement)
