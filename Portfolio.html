About Me

My name is Shathlaine Nicole S. Paragas, and I am 18 years old. I am currently a first-year college student at Our Lady of Fatima University, taking the course Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT). I have always been interested in how technology works, especially in designing and building websites that are both visually appealing and user-friendly.

As a beginner in the field of web development, I am continuously learning and improving my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I enjoy exploring different design styles and experimenting with layouts to create modern and creative websites. Every project I make helps me grow my knowledge and builds my confidence as an aspiring developer.I am passionate about improving my skills in front-end development and UI/UX design, with the goal of becoming a full-stack developer in the future. I want to learn more advanced technologies and frameworks so I can create complete and functional web applications.

Aside from coding, I also value creativity and attention to detail. I believe that a good website is not only about functionality but also about how it makes users feel. That is why I always try to design my work with simplicity, elegance, and purpose. In the future, I hope to gain more experience through projects, collaborations, and continuous learning so I can become a skilled and professional developer in the tech industry.

What I Focus On

I focus on creating modern, responsive, and visually stunning websites that deliver exceptional user experiences. I specialize in designing creative and unique layouts such as asymmetrical, split-screen, Z-shape, and F-shape designs while maintaining clean code, smooth animations, and perfect responsiveness across all devices.
